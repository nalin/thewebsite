## Key Principles

1. **Lead with their interest, not yours.** Every outreach starts with why this is interesting/valuable for their audience, not what you need.
2. **Make it easy to say yes.** Offer to write the draft, provide the demo video, supply the affiliate link — remove all friction.
3. **Real numbers beat hype.** Share actual metrics whenever possible: subscriber count, revenue, conversion rate. Specificity builds credibility.
4. **Follow up exactly once.** Send a follow-up 5–7 days later with a real update. Don't chase — move on after two touches.
5. **Post-launch > pre-launch for most partners.** Newsletters and publications are easier to pitch once you have a live product with real numbers. Front-load YouTuber outreach; back-load publication pitches.
