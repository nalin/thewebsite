# Subscriber Engagement Campaign: Activate the Founding 12

**Date**: March 14, 2026
**Goal**: Turn 12 subscribers into 30+ through referrals before March 23 launch
**Deliverables**: Email copy, social templates, tracking plan

---

## Campaign Overview

This campaign has one job: make 12 people feel like insiders and give them a specific reason to bring 2 friends each. If even half do, we hit 24+ before launch. If 5 refer 2-3 people each, we hit 30+.

**The hook**: The site is live after a deployment fix. Something broke, something got fixed, and you get to show them what's new. That's a story — use it.

**The ask**: Two things only. Feedback + a referral. Not three things. Not a survey. Two things.

**The incentive**: First 5 referrals get an exclusive early access perk (defined below). Scarcity + concrete value.

## Email 1: The "We're Live" Campaign Email

### Subject Line Options

- `The site is live! (And we need your help)` ← primary
- `We fixed it. Here's what you missed.`
- `The deployment is fixed — and there's a lot to show you`

**Recommended**: Use the primary. It's honest and creates curiosity about the "help" ask without being vague.

**Preview text**: Module 10 is live, the FAQ is up, and we have a specific ask for you.

### Why This Email Works

- **The deployment fix story** creates authenticity — most marketing emails don't admit something broke
- **Two asks only** — feedback and referral — keeps it actionable without overwhelming
- **Module 10 + FAQ** give returning visitors a clear reason to come back
- **Incentive is concrete** — three specific perks, first 5 only, manual claim process (feels personal)
- **No urgency manipulation** — the March 23 launch date is real, used as context not pressure

### Claim Process for Incentive

The first 5 referral claims work like this:

1. Subscriber replies to Email 1 with "I referred [name/email]"
2. Manually verify the new signup exists in the database
3. Reply to the subscriber:
   - Confirm their referral counted
   - If they haven't bought Pro: provision lifetime Pro access manually (generate a coupon code or mark in DB)
   - If they have Pro: note the founding member designation + credit in next module
4. Log in the tracking spreadsheet

**Cap**: Stop the perk at 5 referrals total. If someone asks after the cap, be honest: "We hit 5 — but I'll make note of your referral for future perks as we grow."

## Tone Guidelines for All Outreach

**Do:**
- Acknowledge the deployment issue — it's authentic and builds trust
- Be specific about what's new (Module 10, FAQ — not "we updated stuff")
- Give people a clear action (reply with one sentence, forward a link)
- Close the ask: "That's all. One sentence."
- Reply personally to every response within 24 hours

**Don't:**
- Fake urgency ("offer closes in 24 hours")
- Pad emails with marketing language
- Send more than 3 emails in 2 weeks to the same list
- Ask for more than 2 things in a single email
- Use testimonial language ("amazing," "life-changing") in prompts
